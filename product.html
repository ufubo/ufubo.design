Read Project Story & Engineering Details

The Challenge: Designing a next-generation passenger seat required a delicate balance between rigorous safety regulations, weight reduction goals for fuel efficiency, and providing ultimate ergonomic comfort for long-distance travel.

The Solution: Starting from initial sketches, the workflow transitioned rapidly into complex 3D modeling. Every curve was optimized not just for the human body, but for injection molding constraints and assembly line efficiency. Digital prototypes were subjected to rigorous virtual testing before physical mock-ups were created.
